🎯 How to Speak Cantonese (No Writing Needed)

  • 🎵 6 Tones (Jyutping numbers)
    si1 (high flat) · si2 (high rising) · si3 (mid level)
    si4 (low falling) · si5 (low rising) · si6 (low level)
  • 🗣️ Key sounds
    "c" = ts (like "cats") · "z" = ds · "ng" = nasal sound
  • 💬 Question pattern: Raise your tone at the end
    Example: nei5 hou2 maa3? (How are you?)
  • 🔊 Always tap the card + repeat aloud — best way to learn!
  • ✅ Politeness: "m4 goi1" = excuse me / thanks for service
    "do1 ze6" = thank you for a gift or favor
